Asian Trip in 2022

In 2022, I embarked on an unforgettable adventure through Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and South Korea, mesmerized by the unique beauty and cultural richness of each country. From the modern wonders of Singapore's high rise buildings and gigantic Gardens by the Bay, to the harmonious blend of Malaysian tradition and modernity, to the warm hospitality, beautiful landscape, vibrant markets of Thailand, to the romantic and modern of Korea. Each destination gave me a unforgetable memories. As I look back on this incredible journey, I am still feeling excited .
